Pie Sales to begin for Shepherd Baseball

It's pie selling time for the Shepherd baseball Varsity and Junior Varsity players! The pie sales will help pay for players spring break trip to Florida next March. The sales also fund a majority of the program. The pies are made the first Friday and Saturday in December and can be picked up fresh at the cafeteria. Many people buy enough to freeze a few and eat them through out the year.

Blueberry pies sell for $8 each and apple pies are $8 each or 3 for $21. (Prices are subject to change.).

More information to follow. Go JAYS.

From: Shepherd Middle School

Gail Turnwald earns Michigan Council of Teachers (MCTE) Middle School Teacher of the Year Award

Our very own Gail Turnwald has earned the Michigan Council of Teachers of English (MCTE) Middle School Teacher of the Year! It is such an honor to be recognized as best in the state. To receive this recognition, you have to have recommendations and support from your local colleagues, your administrator and your content area state leaders.

It is an honor and a pleasure to work with all of the hard-working, brilliant teachers we have here in Shepherd. Today, let's celebrate one special teacher. Congratulations, Gail Turnwald!
